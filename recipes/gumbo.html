Gumbo

This is how you make a simple chicken and sausage gumbo.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Sausage
  • Onion
  • Peppers
  • Garlic
  • vegetable oil
  • Flour
  • You can add so much more. Shrimp, crab and boiled eggs are some of our favorites.

Directions

  1. Combine oil and flour in a skillet and cook
